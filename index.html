Addressing The Concerns
Safety Has To Be
Part Of The Deal
We understand why parents worry about social media. There are real concerns about talking to strangers, sending things you should not, or people taking advantage of kids online. Honestly, we get that. But Snapchat also has privacy features, parental controls, and friend settings that help make it safer when used responsibly.
🛡️
Family Center
Parents can see who their teenager is talking to without reading private messages.
🔒
Friend Settings

No Random Strangers
We are not adding random strangers or meeting up with people online. We know that is dangerous and stupid.
🔔
Screenshot Alerts
If somebody screenshots something, Snapchat notifies you right away.
